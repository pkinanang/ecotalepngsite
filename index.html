Welcome to Ecotale PNG Limited.

We specialize in sustainable balsa wood harvesting and export. Our mission is to support communities and protect the environment while providing the highest quality timber products.

Ecotale is excited to bring a strong collaborative approach and the empowerment of ENB smallholders of balsa. Our reach into communities revolves around creating employment opportunities for people and their own businesses and the strengthening of local networks. We will be competitive on price and our streamlined processing will ensure quality.

We are market leaders in;

  • Developing competitive export production
  • Encouraging economically viable downstream processing of forest products
  • Ensuring opportunities for the entire community to participate in the development process.
