At Ecotale we measure our success on the value extended to our growers, our focus on delivering the highest quality balsa product is founded on a strong collaborative approach with our farmers. When they succeed, we succeed, when we succeed we all succeed! The balsa wood market is being propelled by the global shift towards renewable energy and the ongoing need for lightweight materials in various industries. We know that China and the broader Asia-Pacific region are currently the largest consumers therefore Ecotale is strategically positioned in Papua New Guinea, specifically in East New Britain to deliver to the region and to the global community
