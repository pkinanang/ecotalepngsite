EXPORT BUSINESS

EcoTale is excited to bring a strong collaborative approach and the empowerment of ENB smallholders of balsa. Our reach into communities revolves around creating employment opportunities for people and their own businesses and the strengthening of local networks. We will be competitive on price and our streamlined processing will ensure quality.

We will be market leaders in;
  • developing competitive export production
  • encouraging economically viable downstream processing of forest products
  • ensuring opportunities for the entire community to participate in the development process.

OUR CLIENTS

Whilts balsa wood production is ongoing, the rapidly increasing demand, especially from the renewable energy sector, places pressure on the supply chain. Therefore, there are times when the supply struggles to keep up with the demand. It is also important to remember that sustainable harvesting practices are very important to the future of the balsa wood market. This growing market demand and the significant issues surrounding global world supply creates a strong opportunity for growing and emerging markets such as Papua New Guinea to rise and contribute to this global supply deficit.

EcoTale is currently creating client opportunities in;
  1. China
  2. Australia
  3. Currently investigating opportunities in the European market
